JoinBrands

JoinBrands is a UGC (User-Generated Content) creator marketplace that
connects brands with content creators to produce marketing materials
such as videos, photos, reviews, and social media posts.

For Brands: - Hire creators quickly at scale - Receive authentic,
ready-to-use marketing content - Use content for ads, social media,
websites, and e-commerce listings - Manage campaigns, approvals,
communication, and payments in one platform

For Creators: - Browse available brand campaigns - Create
product-focused content (unboxing, reviews, lifestyle videos) - Submit
content for approval - Earn money, free products, or both - No large
follower base required

Types of Content: - TikTok/Reels-style short videos - Product reviews
and testimonials - Unboxing videos - Lifestyle photos - Social media ads

Key Benefits: - Authentic content performs better than traditional ads -
Faster and cheaper than professional production - Accessible side income
opportunity for creators - Helps brands scale marketing with real-user
style content

In short: JoinBrands is a platform where companies pay creators to
produce authentic, social-media-style marketing content.
